Who We Are


MediForm wants to adds loads of ease into file accesss and health access by giving you the chance to carry your file everywhere in your phone.



Our Profile


MediForm is a comprehensive mobile application that serves as a country-wide hospital database.
This app aims to facilitate seamless access to healthcare services, enhance the patient experience, and improve healthcare outcomes for individuals residing in the country.



Our Mission


  • 1.To develop a comprehensive, interoperable digital health infrastructure that enables patients to access and control their healthcare records securely, regardless of the provider or location.
  • 2.To provide a user-friendly, patient-centered, and secure system.
  • 3.To give patients access to their medical data effortlessly, fostering better care coordination, informed decision-making, and enhanced patient experiences.


Our Vision


To create a comprehensive, reliable, and user-friendly mobile application that provides seamless access to a countrywide hospital database. Our vision is to empower patients to make informed decisions regarding their healthcare by delivering accurate, up-to-date information about hospitals, healthcare facilities, and medical professionals across the entire country. Through our app, we aim to enhance transparency, improve healthcare access and quality, and ultimately strengthen the overall healthcare system, ensuring that every individual has easy access to the best medical care available.



Our Commitmnet


We are committed to gaining trust from the users and give them a sense of comfort in our app, a friend who's always there. here's how:


  • 1. Accuracy and Reliability: The app needs to ensure that the hospital database is accurate and up-to-date. The commitment includes regularly validating and updating the database to reflect changes in hospital information, changes in contact details, specialties offered, etc..
  • 2. User Privacy and Security: This commitment involves implementing robust encryption techniques to protect the stored data, adhering to strict privacy policies, and following industry best practices in terms of data protection.
  • 3. User-friendly Interface and Features: We commit to providing a user-friendly interface and valuable features to enhance user experience. This commitment includes designing an intuitive and easy-to-navigate interface.
